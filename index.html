Why Join Vibe?

Born in the Silicon Valley of India , Vibe seeks to bring together users using audio-centred features such as public & private Vibe rooms, an easy-to-use drop-in/drop-out Walkie-Talkie, and more.

Add your hobbies and areas of interest to your profile, then turn on Vibe Mode to try and find a Vibe that matches yours (or even a new vibe if you’re feeling adventurous).You get to choose who to Vibe with (or who not to Vibe with).

We are all about finding good Vibes and bringing them together

Our Vision

After experiencing the online only communication due
to the current situation of COVID. We believe
physical/offline communication is very important for any
relationship, be it family, friends or colleugues. Our
vision is to help social media compliemnt the physical
intimacy to relationships and communication.
